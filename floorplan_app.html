📖 How to Use This App

A simple step-by-step guide — read at your own pace

Step 1 — Load Your Equipment List
1
Click "Load Equipment List" at the top of the screen. Find and select the CSV file that contains your equipment. The list will appear on the left side of the screen automatically.
2
If you don't have a CSV file, you can type equipment in manually using the form on the left. Fill in the name, choose a type, and click "+ Add to Library".
Step 2 — Place Equipment on the Map
3
Click and hold any equipment name from the left list, then drag it onto the map and let go where you want it placed.
4
You can also just click an equipment name — it will appear in the middle of the map. You can move it from there.
5
To move something already on the map, click and drag it to a new position.
6
To remove something from the map, double-click on it (click it twice quickly). It will disappear.
Step 3 — Navigate the Map
7
Use the scroll wheel on your mouse to zoom in and out. You can also use the ＋ and － buttons at the bottom.
8
Click the "✋ Pan" button at the bottom to switch into "move map" mode. Then click and drag the map to move around. Click Pan again to go back to normal.
9
Click "⊙ Fit" to reset the view and show the whole map on screen.
Step 4 — Save & Share Your Work
10
Click "💾 Save My Work" to download a save file to your computer. You can reload this later with "📂 Open Saved Work" — nothing will be lost.
11
Click "📸 Save as Picture" to download a photo of the current map — great for sharing with others via email or printing.
12
Click "📄 Export List" to download a file listing all placed equipment with their positions.
Tips
★
Your work is automatically saved in the browser as you go. If you close and reopen the page, everything should still be there.
★
Use the filter buttons on the left to show only certain types of equipment (e.g. only HVAC, only Batteries).
★
Use "⊞ Snap" to make items automatically line up on a grid — useful for keeping things neat.
★
Hover your mouse over any item on the map to see its full details in a pop-up.
